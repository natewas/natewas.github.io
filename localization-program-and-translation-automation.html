Localization Program

TL;DR: I designed and built an automated localization system to make MasterControl Academy course content available in 15 languages. The solution integrates a custom pre- and post-processing utility that parses SCORM packages, leverages SmartCat’s command-line interface (CLI) for translation, and reinserts the content—enabling full course localization (text, UI, captions, and media) within 24 hours. The result: drastically reduced manual effort, improved compliance, and a scalable foundation for global training success.

Challenge

MasterControl Academy offers 750+ English-language eLearning courses, but lacked a scalable localization process. The existing manual workflow took 38–60 minutes per course and left key components untranslated, including:

  • Video captions remaining in English.
  • Internal navigation and links redirecting to untranslated content.
  • Poor searchability for non-English terms.

These limitations created barriers for non-native speakers and posed compliance risks in regulated global markets.

Impact

  • 96 courses localized, scaling to 750+
  • Up to 80% reduction in localization time
  • Automated full course translation
  • Adopted globally as the standard
