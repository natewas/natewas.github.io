About me

I'm a lifelong learner, problem-solver, and designer. For the past four years, I’ve been part of the education team at a Life Sciences company, most recently as a program coordinator, helping customers bring life-changing products to market. My background blends instructional technology, psychology, design thinking, and even music (because picking just one path sounded way too limiting).

I thrive on discovering and designing smarter solutions, whether that’s in education, technology, or my latest woodworking project. I believe in automating the tedious, embracing the messy parts of creativity, and creating space for unfiltered ideas. When I’m not optimizing, researching, or tinkering, you’ll find me fostering beagles with my wife and our own beagle, Walter, or deep in a new learning rabbit hole—probably on YouTube.
